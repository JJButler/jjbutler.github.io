THISTLE INSURANCE


Thistle Insurance's Compass Scheme provides caravan parks with the option to offer insurance directly to customers to cover their static caravan, leisure lodge, chalet or park home.

Thistle wanted an easy-to-use extranet portal for park owners, as appointed representatives to let them administer the insurance and issue documentation.

Building on our platform, SCS delivered a bespoke cloud service through a short, agile development project where the Thistle team were involved in specifying and testing the functionality as it was built.

The result is an online service that provides the user interface that the park owners wanted, combined with the administrative backend that Thistle needed.
